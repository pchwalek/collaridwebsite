The Mission: Beyond GPS

Traditional GPS tells us where an animal goes, but leaves behavior and environmental context ambiguous. CollarID closes the data gap.

Holistic Insight

Fusing location with high-fidelity bioacoustics and inertial metrics to understand the complete behavioral picture.

Microclimatic Context

Recording temperature, humidity, and air pressure second-by-second to capture the environmental conditions animals experience.

Novel Air Quality Sensing

The first animal-borne logger to measure particulate matter (wildfire smoke and pollutants) directly from the animal's perspective.

Location-Only Data

  • Limited behavioral context
  • Ambiguous environmental conditions
  • No direct health indicators

Holistic Sensing

  • GPS + Behavioral + Environmental data
  • Real-time microclimatic context
  • Direct air quality exposure metrics

Engineering Excellence

Built for extreme durability and long-term autonomous operation in the harshest wilderness environments.

Mechanical Housing

Designed for extreme durability. Validated using Finite Element Analysis (FEA) against the 1,768 N bite force of a lion.

Durability Validated

Proven waterproof and resilient through extreme environmental tests, including a dishwasher stress test. Polycarbonate top and anodized aluminum base provide optimal protection.

Power Autonomy

Integrated 154 mW solar harvesting paired with an intelligent hibernation protocol for multi-year deployments.

Communication

Low-power, long-range LoRaWAN telemetry with planned Argos Satellite integration for true global autonomy.

Multi-Modal Sensors

GPS, high-fidelity bioacoustics, IMU, temperature, humidity, pressure, and particulate matter sensing.

Finite Element Analysis

FEA simulations validate structural integrity against extreme forces, ensuring the device can withstand the physical demands of tracking wild animals like lions and hyenas.
